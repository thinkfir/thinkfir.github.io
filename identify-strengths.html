Identify Your Strengths and Interests

  • The first step towards working a part-time job in high school is choosing a job.
  • What are you good at? What are you passionate about? These questions will help you find the right type of job.
  • Think about your skills, hobbies, and interests. Do you enjoy working with people, solving problems, or being creative?
  • Some ideas to help you learn more about yourself are online self tests like a personality quiz, learning styles survey, or interests test.
  • Money is often a motivating factor into getting a part time job, so if you need it make sure that your job pays you enough money so that you will be financially stable.
  • Once you know your strengths, you can focus your search on jobs that match your skills and interests.
Have fun, but take your time identifiying your skills and passion. Remember, you should always pick a part-time job that you have an intrest in or feel comfortable working in, and never pick a job purely for money.
