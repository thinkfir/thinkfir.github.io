Project Confidence and Professionalism

  • When talking, make eye contact, speak clearly and confidently and ask thoughful questions to show your employer that you are actively engaged in the conversation.
  • Use effective body language, as your body conveys more than your words. Show enthusiasm and a genuine interest in the job.
  • Prevent using filler words like "umm", "ahh", "whatever", and "uhhh". Instead of using filler words, used timed pauses that create more emphasis and gravitas in your speech.
  • When writing, use professional and readable fonts, and always be polite and sincere.
Be as professional as possible! Good luck!
